About Me

I am a degree qualified Electronic Engineer based near Southampton, UK who is developing software programming skills with the ambition of changing career.

Having graduated at Southampton University, I have spent 10 years in the Space industry first as a Systems engineer working on Telecommunication satellites, now as a Senior engineer working in the SatCom sector repairing ground station communication amplifiers.

Having enjoyed doing some programming in my professional roles, using Python and VBA, I started to do some self learning and small projects in my spare time. In the last 18 months I have been spending a lot of my free time coding, using Udemy courses, books and online resources to learn a variety of different languages and frameworks. I have used this growing knowledge to create a variety of exciting and substantial projects using software and hardware.

Since the beginning of 2020 I went part time to further accelerate my learning. I have a real passion for using technologies to solve problems and improve peoples lives and a real drive to continue to grow and develop my skills. I am actively looking to start my professional career in software development, so if you would like to find out more please contact me.
